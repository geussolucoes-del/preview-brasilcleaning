Privacy Policy

How BrasilCleaning handles your information.

This policy explains what we collect through our website and estimate form, why we collect it, and how you can contact us about your information.

Last updated: July 16, 2026

Information we collect

When you request an estimate, we may collect your name, phone number, email address, ZIP code, preferred contact method, service type, property details, preferred start date and any notes you choose to provide.

We may also collect basic website usage information, such as pages visited, form events and device/browser information, to understand how people use the site and improve the experience.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your cleaning estimate request.
  • To contact you by text, phone or email based on your preference.
  • To schedule, quote and provide cleaning services.
  • To improve our website, form and customer communication.
  • To protect our business, clients and website from misuse.

Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers that help us operate the website, process form submissions, communicate with clients or deliver services. These providers should use the information only to support BrasilCleaning.

Cookies and analytics

Our website may use necessary cookies for basic functionality and optional analytics cookies to understand site performance. You can review details in our Cookie Policy.

Data retention

We keep estimate and client communication information for as long as needed to respond to requests, provide services, maintain business records and meet legal obligations.

Your choices

You can ask us to update, correct or delete information you provided, subject to reasonable business and legal requirements. You can also ask us to stop contacting you for non-essential communication.

Children's privacy

Our services and website are intended for adults requesting cleaning services. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact

For privacy questions, call or text BrasilCleaning at (954) 274-2055. You may also contact Rosangela Souza through the same number.

This policy is a practical website privacy notice and should be reviewed by legal counsel if BrasilCleaning needs jurisdiction-specific legal compliance wording.
